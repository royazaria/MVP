COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

How does ePro AI actually work?

Upload a single product photo, and our AI analyzes your image, then taps into our proprietary database of winning Etsy listings. The system identifies what's working in your specific category and generates optimized titles, descriptions, tags, and images specifically designed to maximize visibility and sales.

How much time will this actually save me?

Our average user reports saving 7-10 hours every week! If you're adding just one new product daily, you're currently spending 1-2 hours per listing on photography, editing, keyword research, and writing descriptions. ePro AI reduces this entire process to 2-5 minutes per listing.

What does a token actually get me?

Tokens are your flexible currency within ePro AI. Each token equals $1 USD. Our Standard plan (1 token) delivers SEO-optimized titles, descriptions and tags, while Professional (2 tokens) adds 5 studio-quality images, and Premium (5 tokens) includes 8 images plus a product creation video.

Can I edit the content after it's generated?

Yes! While most users find our AI-generated content ready to use immediately, you have complete control to personalize any aspect. Our system provides you with editable text files and high-resolution images that you can tweak before uploading to Etsy.

Is the AI optimized for specific Etsy categories?

Absolutely! Our AI has been trained on successful listings across all major Etsy categories. When you select your product category, the system specifically tailors its output to match what performs best in that niche, including category-specific keywords and presentation styles.

How are the product images generated?

Our advanced AI imaging technology creates professional studio-quality images by analyzing your original product photo. It maintains the integrity of your product while enhancing lighting, adding appropriate backgrounds, and even placing it in lifestyle contexts or on models when relevant to your product type.

What's the difference between the plans?

The Standard plan (1 token) provides text content only - optimized title, description, and tags. Professional (2 tokens) adds 5 high-quality product images with various backgrounds. Premium (5 tokens) includes 8 ultra-realistic images with lifestyle contexts plus a short product creation video that showcases craftsmanship.

Is there a limit to how many listings I can create?

There's no limit! You can create as many listings as you have tokens for. Our most active sellers typically purchase our Pro package (250 tokens) which allows for creating 125 Professional listings. Unused tokens never expire, so you can use them at your own pace.

How up-to-date is the SEO optimization?

Our system connects to real-time Etsy search data through EtsyHunt API, ensuring your listings are optimized for current search trends and buyer behavior. The AI constantly learns from the latest successful listings to keep your content competitive in the marketplace.

Can I use this for bulk listings?

Yes! Our CSV upload feature allows you to process up to 50 products at once. Simply follow our template format with your product images, basic descriptions, materials, sizes, and categories. The system will process each item individually with the same quality and optimization as single uploads.
